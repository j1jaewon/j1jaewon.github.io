# AI Strengths and Limitations — Personal Assessment

Based on hands-on experience with ChatGPT in professional TBT/regulation and newsletter workflows.

## Strengths
- **Summarization**: Consistently good at condensing long documents into key points
- **Report drafting**: Can produce structured first drafts quickly
- **Information structuring**: Organizes unstructured content into formatted output
- **Multilingual handling**: Translates government gazettes from many languages reliably

## Limitations
- **Human writing style**: Cannot replicate natural, human-sounding prose
  — output often feels formulaic or AI-generated
- **Flexible reasoning**: Rule-based behavior limits handling of ambiguous or nuanced situations
- **Author vs. reader mode**: Tends to insert editor-facing comments ("원문에 따르면", 
  "검토 필요") into final output rather than staying in "finished document" mode
- **Consistency across iterations**: Small prompt changes can produce unpredictably different results

## Key Practical Insight
> "AI가 요약을 정말 잘하고 보고서 초안도 잘 잡는데, 인간의 문체로 글을 작성한다거나
> 룰베이스로 작동하기 때문에 유연한 사고를 하기에는 부족한 점이 있어"

The sweet spot: use AI for **structure and speed**, apply human judgment for **tone and nuance**.
