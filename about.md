Hi, I'm Jaewon Jeong.

I am a researcher at the Korea Testing Laboratory (KTL), working on the analysis of overseas technical regulations and certification systems. My work focuses on helping industries understand regulatory requirements and respond effectively in global markets.

Before joining KTL, I was involved in R&D planning and standardization in the display industry, where I worked on national projects and international standardization activities, including IEC technical committees.

My interests lie at the intersection of technology, regulation, and industrial policy. I am particularly interested in how technical standards and regulatory frameworks influence global trade and industrial competitiveness.

I aim to bridge gaps between technical systems, regulatory environments, and real-world industry needs.
